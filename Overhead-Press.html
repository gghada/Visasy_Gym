Overhead Press 
Stand upright and keep the back straight. Hold a dumbbell in each hand, 
at the shoulders, with an overhand grip. Thumbs are on the inside 
and knuckles face up.

Exhale as you raise the weights above the head in a controlled motion.

Pause briefly at the top of the motion.

Inhale and return the dumbbells to the shoulders.
Duration
60s
